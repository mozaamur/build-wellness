Vitamin A

Since vitamin A is fat-soluble, your body can store it. You can get it from a variety of foods, including carrots and cantaloupe, so if you follow a healthy diet, you probably get enough of it. However, people who have specific medical conditions are more vulnerable to deficiencies and may benefit from taking a supplement.
